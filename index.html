Unveiling Our Temple

Welcome to our story, welcome to us.

    The Thiruvadhigai Natyanjali event, an annual celebration held at the Panruti Thiruvathigai Veerateeswarar Temple, marks a cultural extravaganza deeply rooted in tradition. This enchanting event unfolds every year on Shiva Rathri, drawing classical dancers, dedicated crews, and revered gurus from every corner of Tamil Nadu. The significance of this occasion is heightened by its three-day duration, with a focal point on the auspicious night of Shiva Rathri. The Panruti Thiruvathigai Veerateeswarar Temple stands as a venerable backdrop to this cultural spectacle. Nestled in the heart of Panruti, this sacred shrine holds historical and religious importance, contributing to the spiritual ambiance of the Thiruvadhigai Natyanjali event. Devotees and performers alike are immersed in the divine atmosphere, fostering a unique connection between art and spirituality. For over a decade, the Thiruvadhigai Natyanjali event has triumphed as a testament to cultural preservation and artistic excellence. Its success is not merely measured in years but in the harmonious convergence of dance, devotion, and tradition. It is a sacred edifice that encapsulates centuries of architectural grandeur and spiritual significance.
The event has evolved into a platform that transcends regional boundaries, attracting participants and enthusiasts who recognize the sanctity of the occasion. During these three days, the temple grounds resonate with the rhythmic footfalls of classical dancers, echoing the rich heritage of Tamil Nadu's traditional art forms. The performances range from soul-stirring solo acts to mesmerizing group presentations, showcasing the diversity and depth of Indian classical dance. Shiva Rathri, the pinnacle of the event, witnesses a spiritual crescendo as artists pay homage to Lord Shiva through their dance. The vibrant costumes, intricate choreography, and soulful expressions create an immersive experience for the audience, fostering a connection between the earthly and the divine. As the Thiruvadhigai Natyanjali event continues to flourish, it not only honors the cultural legacy of Tamil Nadu but also serves as a dynamic platform for the preservation and propagation of classical dance forms. This annual celebration stands as a beacon, drawing artists, patrons, and enthusiasts into a shared journey of artistic expression and spiritual awakening at the revered Panruti Thiruvathigai Veerateeswarar Temple.

Seamless Procedures Unveiled

Experience efficiency with our streamlined procedures.

  • The time allotted in Thiruvadhigai Natyanjali is as follows Solo – 20 mins, Group – 30 mins, Ballet – 45 mins
  • Group Performers upto 15 members allowed.
  • The dance items should pertain to traditional themes related to the lord.
  • Only live Orchestra is permitted in sivarathri day.
  • Thiruvadhigai Natyanjali Committee can arrange Accompanists (ie-Vocal,Mridangam,violin etc.) for the Artistes at nominal cost if needed.
  • The dancers should follow their timings very strictly.
  • Local Accommodation, Boarding & Transport from the venue will be provided on the day of performance
  • There will be no remuneration or travelling allowance.
  • We have organized to completely record your programme both in video and photos. No private photo / video graphers are allowed.
  • Artists willing to have their own accommodation in Hotels or with their Relatives are welcome to do so.
  • Panruti is 20 kms from Villupuram and 50 Kms from Pondicherry, 160Kms from Chennai . Trains, Bus are available from Chennai.
  • Any of your requests over the phone shall also be confirmed in writing to make co-ordination and arrangements possible.
  • Any further information in this regard can be given to you at any time.
Guidelines

Dive into our guidelines for a clear understanding of expectations and a smooth journey ahead.

  • Free Accommodation will be available to the Participants on Prior Request.
  • Your stay is confirmed for 24hrs only. If you want to extend your stay, arrangements can be made at your cost.
  • Accompanying Persons will be treated as Guests. They can be accommodated only on Payment of charges.
  • Accommodation & Food will be provided opposite to the temple. No room service is provided. Break Fast Time 8.00AM-9.30AM, Lunch Time 1.00PM-2.30PM, Dinner 7.30PM 9.00PM
  • Every Participant is presented with a Memento and Certificate.
  • Don’t use the room for makeup purposes. Make use of the green room provided in the venue
  • Make sure to be in the venue 1 Hour ahead of your schedule.
  • Small Green room and refreshment will be available at the temple premises.
  • No Toilet facilities available inside the Temple Premises.
  • Please produce our identity card for ready recognition, at all times.
